The development of decentralized identity systems has made significant progress in recent years. Particularly interesting is the approach of using Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) as carriers of identity information.

An NFT-based identity system offers several decisive advantages:

  • Self-sovereign control over your own data
  • Immutable traceability
  • Interoperability between different platforms
  • Protection against identity theft

The AEra Identity System exemplifies how such a solution can work in practice. Through the combination of SIWE (Sign-In with Ethereum) and NFT verification, a robust authentication system emerges that is both secure and user-friendly.

Particularly noteworthy is the "Resonance Score" system, which measures user interaction with the ecosystem and rewards it. This creates incentives for active participation while enabling differentiated access control.

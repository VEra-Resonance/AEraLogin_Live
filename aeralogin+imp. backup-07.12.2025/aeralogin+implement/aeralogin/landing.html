‚öôÔ∏è How It Works

The complete user journey from signup to on-chain reputation

1. Wallet Connect & Signup

Users connect their wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect). The system generates a challenge message that the user signs. No passwords, no emails - your wallet is your identity.

2. Identity NFT Minting (Gasless)

On first login, users automatically receive a Soul-Bound Identity NFT. Minting is completely gasless - users pay nothing! Gas costs are covered by our community through donations. The NFT is non-transferable and serves as permanent on-chain identity.

üíô Support the community: Help us onboard more users by donating to our community wallet

3. Social Interactions & Scoring

Users can follow others, share content, and engage. Each interaction increases the score in the database (instant) and is stored in the Registry Contract (on-chain verification).

4. Resonance Calculation

The Resonance Score combines Own Score with average Follower Scores. Creates a network effect: Creators with engaged followers have higher Resonance. Example: Own 54 + Avg Follower 66 = Total Resonance 120.

5. Automatic Blockchain Sync

A background process syncs scores to blockchain every 30 seconds. Milestone system: Sync occurs every 2 points (e.g. 50 ‚Üí 52 ‚Üí 54). Users see live status in dashboard: "‚úÖ Synced" or "‚è≥ Pending (+X)".

6. Dashboard & Analytics

Users have access to a complete dashboard with: Follower list, NFT status, Blockchain Score comparison, Interaction history, and direct links to BaseScan for every transaction.
